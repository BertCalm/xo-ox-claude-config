## Strategic Decisions — March 14, 2026

### 1. V1 Scope: Everything today is V1
- All existing engines, features, concepts, and work-in-progress as of 2026-03-14 EOD = V1
- Nothing is deferred to V2 that exists today
- Only new ideas from 2026-03-15 onward can be V2
- This means: 24 working engines + 5 Constellation stubs (once fixed) + 4 concept engines = all V1
- **Why:** The expanding universe IS the product. Shipping a subset would misrepresent the vision.

### 3. Site: HT Has Creative Control
- Light mode is dominant for the instrument/plugin UI — flexible, not rigid
- The site is HT Ammell's domain — dark ocean aesthetic is approved
- Update the brand spec to acknowledge: "Plugin UI: Gallery Model light mode default. Site: Deep Gallery variant, HT Ammell creative direction."
- **How to apply:** Stop flagging the dark site as a brand violation. It's intentional.

### 4. Seance Reframing: Possessed Vessels
- The legends don't speak directly — they possess human vessels who interpret the wisdom
- Vessels apply the insights in context and become "quoteable" as characters
- We pay homage by marking "inspired by the principles/ideas/revelations/breakthroughs of [Pioneer Name]"
- The lore and history sections should flesh out the real contributions of each pioneer
- Quotes are attributed to the character/vessel, not the historical person
- **Why:** Respects the legacies while maintaining creative freedom. Avoids false endorsement.
- **How to apply:** Update the Synth Seance skill, Oracle blog post, Signal posts, and any future content to use this framing.
