### Key Architecture
- **String Engine**: Karplus-Strong waveguide, 4 materials (Nylon/Steel/Crystal/Light)
- **Microsound Engine**: Per-voice granular fragmentation (Stutter/Scatter/Freeze/Reverse)
- **Note-Based Crossover**: Voices route to LOW or HIGH FX path based on pitch
- **FX Path LOW (The Deep)**: Sub harmonic → Tape sat → Dark delay → Deep plate reverb
- **FX Path HIGH (The Light)**: Shimmer → Micro delay → Spectral smear → Crystal chorus
- **~40 parameters**, 16 voices, oldest-note stealing, no legato
- **4 Macros**: PLUCK (string character), FRACTURE (microsound intensity), SURFACE (crossover split), DIVINE (shimmer/resonance/space)
- **Sympathetic resonance**: Tuned comb filter bank modeling unplucked strings
