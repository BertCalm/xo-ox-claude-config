# B008 — XOppossum's Five-Macro System

*Blessed: 2026-03-14 | Engine: XOppossum (BITE) | Ghosts: All 8*

## The Blessed Feature

XOppossum has 5 named macros — BELLY, BITE, SCURRY, TRASH, PLAY DEAD — breaking the XOmnibus convention of 4 macros (M1-M4). Each macro tells a survival story: BELLY adds warmth and weight, BITE adds aggression and harmonics, SCURRY increases movement and modulation, TRASH pushes into lo-fi destruction, PLAY DEAD reduces everything to near-silence.

## Why It Is Protected

The ghosts unanimously declared this the best macro system in the XO_OX fleet. The naming convention turns parameter bundles into emotional gestures — a musician who has never read a manual can push BITE and immediately understand what happens. The 5th macro (PLAY DEAD) is unprecedented: a macro designed to make the instrument *stop* rather than *go*. This inverts the assumption that all performance controls should add energy. With 342 presets all responding to these 5 axes, the system is proven at scale.

## Do Not

- Reduce to 4 macros to match XOmnibus convention (the adapter will handle the mapping)
- Rename to generic M1-M5 labels
- Remove PLAY DEAD — the "do less" macro is the most original idea
