# B004 — XOverdub's Spring Reverb

*Blessed: 2026-03-14 | Engine: XOverdub | Ghost: Vangelis, Isao Tomita*

## The Blessed Feature

XOverdub's spring reverb emulation — the splashy, metallic, characterful reverb that gives the dub engine its sense of physical space. Unlike algorithmic plate or hall reverbs, the spring reverb has a specific mechanical character: the metallic "sproing," the frequency-dependent decay, the way transients splash through the spring and emerge transformed.

## Why It Is Protected

The spring reverb IS the dub sound. Without it, XOverdub is a delay pedal. With it, XOverdub is a space — a room made of metal and tape and warm amplifier hum. The ghosts praised its character specifically because it doesn't sound "clean" or "polished." It sounds like a real spring in a real amp.

## Do Not

- Replace with a generic algorithmic reverb
- "Improve" by making it sound cleaner or more neutral
- Remove the metallic character in pursuit of smoothness
