# B002 — Onset's XVC Cross-Voice Coupling

*Blessed: 2026-03-14 | Engine: Onset | Ghost: Ikutaro Kakehashi, All 8*

## The Blessed Feature

The XVC (Cross-Voice Coupling) system — an 8x8 modulation matrix where each drum voice's peak amplitude can modulate parameters of every other voice. This is declared "3-5 years ahead of the industry" and represents Onset's most original contribution to drum synthesis.

## Why It Is Protected

XVC is what makes Onset more than a drum machine — it makes it a drum *organism*. The inter-voice modulation creates emergent rhythmic behavior that transcends individual voice programming. No competing drum engine offers formalized cross-voice modulation as a first-class system.

## Prism Sweep Implementation (Round 10D)

**12 XVC demo presets written.** Each preset in the fleet exercises at least one XVC pair, making B002 demonstrable at first load without reading documentation.

Full guide and preset descriptions: `Docs/onset_xvc_demo_guide.md`

Preset naming (elevated in Round 11H): Neural Storm, Solar System, Gravity Bend, and 9 others — all named after physical phenomena where cross-object interaction produces emergent behavior.

Buchla, on the demo presets: *"The map should be discoverable by touch. Now it is."*

## Do Not

- Simplify XVC to a fixed routing (the 8x8 matrix IS the feature)
- Remove XVC to save CPU (optimize it instead)
- Replace with sidechain compression ("that's XVC for cowards" — Buchla)
- Hide XVC behind an "advanced" panel (it should be front and center)
