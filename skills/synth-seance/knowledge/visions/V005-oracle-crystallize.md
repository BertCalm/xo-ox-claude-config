This transforms Oracle from a generative instrument into a discovery tool where the musician mines the stochastic field for gems.

## Why It Matters

Middle Eastern and North African maqam traditions are living oral traditions that resist Western notation. Oracle's combination of mathematical unpredictability (GENDY) with cultural structure (maqam scales) creates a unique space where tradition and innovation coexist. The crystallize function would let performers preserve discoveries without taming the generator.

A freeze buffer that captures N cycles of GENDY output as a wavetable, playable as a static waveform until unfrozen. The captured moment becomes a preset within a preset — a discovered timbre that can be recalled.
