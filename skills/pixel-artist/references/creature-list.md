# XO_OX Creature Roster — Tier 2 Pixel Art System

**71 engines. Pixel art style (16x16 or 32x32). One creature per engine.**

Each engine has a dedicated creature mascot rendered in pixel art. These are Tier 2 identities — distinct from the feliX/Oscar hero pair (Tier 1). Used in the web aquarium, pack artwork, and Guru Bin materials.

> **Broth Quad overlap note**: Three Broth Quad engines (OVERWASH #21, OVERWORN #22, OVERCAST #23) are listed under the Thermocline zone where they thematically belong. The fourth Broth engine, OVERFLOW #65, appears in the Kitchen Collection section. All four are part of the Kitchen Collection — Broth Quad.
